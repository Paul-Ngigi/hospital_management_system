Nazareth Ruiru Hospital

Nazareth Ruiru Hospital is a branch of Nazareth Hospital in Kiambu .
Nazareth Hospital is a mission Hospital under the Catholic Archdiocese of Nairobi.
The Nazareth Ruiru Hospital was started as a Dispensary in August 2009.
The official opening took place on 15th August 2009 and was graced by many Ruiru leaders and community members.
